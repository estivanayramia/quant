# Data Source Policy

QuantOS is cache-first and local-first.

- No paid data is required.
- No broker or exchange trading keys are required.
- CI must not fetch data from the internet.
- Large datasets and proprietary downloads must not be committed.
- Source and license notes are required for historical imports.
- Historical data may be used for research evidence only.
- Synthetic data and historical data must be labeled separately.

Live promotion remains blocked even when historical data quality checks pass.
